Meet Your Therapist

Liza Abreu

Licensed Massage Therapist

With over 23 years of experience, Liza began her journey in the heart of Miami, Florida — refining her craft at some of the most prestigious luxury hotels on Miami Beach. That foundation of excellence, care, and attention to detail is something she brings to every single session.

Liza specializes in spa rituals designed for the mind, body & soul — from targeted chronic area work and full body muscular tension relief to deep stress reduction treatments. Whether you're seeking relaxation, rehabilitation, or simply a moment of peace, Liza tailors every session specifically to you.

"Everyone deserves to be Pampered!"

Her commitment is simple: to provide professional, personalized care in a warm, cozy, and safe environment where you always leave feeling better than when you arrived.
