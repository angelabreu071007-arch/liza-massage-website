Prenatal Bliss

A specialized pregnancy massage providing safe, nurturing relief for the shoulders, lower back, and hips — the areas that carry the most during pregnancy.

About This Treatment

Care for Two

Prenatal Bliss is a deeply nurturing massage designed specifically for expectant mothers. Pregnancy places unique demands on the body — shifting posture, added weight, hormonal changes, and heightened sensitivity all require a therapist with specialized training and a genuinely attentive touch.

Liza's prenatal approach prioritizes safety above all. Positioning, pressure, and technique are all carefully adapted for each trimester. The focus areas — shoulders, lower back, hips, and legs — are precisely where pregnancy tension accumulates most, and where thoughtful therapeutic work brings the most relief.

This 75-minute session closes with a luxurious foot scrub, leaving tired, swollen feet refreshed and deeply cared for. Clients consistently describe Prenatal Bliss as one of the most restorative experiences of their pregnancy.

Safe for all trimesters. Please let Liza know your current trimester when booking so your session can be tailored appropriately. Always consult your healthcare provider if you have any concerns.

What's Included

  • Specialized prenatal massage safe for all trimesters
  • Focus on shoulders, lower back, hips, and legs
  • Pregnancy-safe positioning and pressure throughout
  • Complimentary luxurious foot scrub
  • Warm towel treatment and relaxing aromatherapy
