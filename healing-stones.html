About This Treatment

The Power of Thermal Therapy

Healing Stones is a deeply therapeutic treatment that harnesses the power of temperature contrast to work where other massages can't reach. Smooth volcanic basalt stones, heated to the perfect temperature, are placed along the body's energy pathways while Liza uses them as tools to apply firm, purposeful pressure.

The alternating application of hot and cold stones creates a pumping effect in the circulatory system — drawing blood into stiff muscles, flushing out toxins, and dramatically reducing inflammation. It's particularly effective for chronic back pain, stiff joints, and deep-seated muscular tension.

By the end of this 90-minute journey, your muscles will feel genuinely released — not just relaxed on the surface, but restored at a deeper level.
