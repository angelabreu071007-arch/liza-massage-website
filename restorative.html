About This Treatment

Deep Relief, Your Way

The Restorative session is built entirely around you. Before the session begins, Liza takes time to understand your specific areas of concern — whether that's chronic lower back pain, tight shoulders from desk work, sports recovery, or general muscular fatigue. The treatment is then designed from the ground up to address exactly what your body needs.

Deep tissue techniques work into the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue, breaking up adhesions and releasing long-held tension patterns. Cupping therapy uses gentle suction to lift the fascia, increase blood flow, and accelerate the body's natural healing process — often reaching areas that hands alone can't access.

The 90-minute upgrade adds bamboo tool work — firm, heated bamboo rods that provide an exceptionally deep and thorough massage, leaving muscles thoroughly relaxed and restored.

What's Included

  • Customized deep tissue massage targeting your specific concerns
  • Cupping therapy for fascial release and circulation boost
  • Pre-session consultation to map your treatment plan
  • 90-min option adds bamboo tool massage for deeper relief
